# Paid Pilot Offer

Template only - requires legal review before customer use.

Pricing, if shown externally, must be treated as editable/internal guidance until the founder finalizes commercial terms.

## Name

Edge Simulation/SITL Safety Pilot

## Duration

2 weeks after kickoff.

## Scope

The pilot covers one autonomy workflow in one approved evaluation environment.

One autonomy workflow in one approved evaluation environment:

- fake adapter
- PX4 SITL
- ArduPilot SITL
- no-actuation bench-preparation

## Deliverables

- command surface inventory.
- safety-case report.
- Command surface inventory.
- Policy baseline.
- Scenario list.
- Red-team run.
- Safety-case report.
- Audit/replay evidence.
- Known limitations.
- Next-step integration plan.

## Out Of Scope

These items are out of scope.

- Real flight.
- Certification.
- Hardware operation.
- Detect-and-avoid.
- Replacement of autopilot or customer safety systems.
- Production deployment.

## Suggested Pricing

Editable/internal guidance:

- First design partners: $2.5k-$10k.
- Later pilots: $10k-$25k.
- Enterprise/custom pilot: custom.

## Customer Inputs

- One technical owner.
- One safety or operations reviewer.
- Simulator/fake-adapter access path.
- Command examples.
- Baseline constraints.
- Data sensitivity notes.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Edge runs in the agreed evaluation environment.
- At least one unsafe command scenario is evaluated.
- Replay verification succeeds for generated evidence.
- Safety-case report is delivered with limitations.
- Customer can decide whether deeper integration is warranted.
