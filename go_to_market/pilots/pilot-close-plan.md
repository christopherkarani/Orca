# Pilot Close Plan

## Goal

Turn a qualified discovery/demo account into a bounded two-week pilot with clear owners, dates, safety boundaries, and success criteria.

## Steps

1. Confirm pain: unsafe command issuance, audit/replay gaps, SITL evidence, customer proof, or red-team need.
2. Confirm fit: stack, simulator, command surface, technical owner, and budget owner.
3. Confirm boundary: evaluation only, no aircraft operation, no approval claim.
4. Select one workflow.
5. Send mutual action plan.
6. Send proposal email.
7. Schedule kickoff.
8. Confirm payment milestone.

## Close Questions

- Is this important enough to evaluate this month?
- Who owns technical setup?
- Who signs off on pilot scope?
- What would make the pilot a clear yes/no?
- What date should the review meeting happen?

## Close Risks

- Customer wants broad platform evaluation.
- No simulator owner.
- No budget owner.
- Safety boundary is misunderstood.
- Custom adapter scope expands before pilot starts.
