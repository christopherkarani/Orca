# Qualification Framework

Score one point for each yes.

## Questions

1. Do they use autonomous mission planning?
2. Do agents or planners issue commands?
3. Do they use MAVLink, PX4, ArduPilot, ROS2, MAVROS, or a command bridge?
4. Do they have simulation/SITL?
5. Do they need audit/replay evidence?
6. Do they have geofence, altitude, velocity, battery, or mode constraints?
7. Do they have operator approval requirements?
8. Do they worry about unsafe command issuance?
9. Do they need to show evidence to customers, regulators, operators, or internal safety teams?
10. Can they run a 2-week pilot without aircraft deployment?

## Scoring

- 0-2: Poor fit.
- 3-5: Possible fit.
- 6-8: Strong fit.
- 9-10: Ideal first pilot.

## Disqualify If

- They only want certified aircraft deployment now.
- They have no simulation/SITL path.
- They want weapons/kinetic enablement.
- They expect Aegis to replace autopilot or detect-and-avoid behavior.
- They cannot participate in technical discovery.
- They ask to bypass failsafes, hide evidence, or remove limitations language.
