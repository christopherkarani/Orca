I am looking for 3 drone/autonomy design partners for Edge.

Edge is a local safety-policy and audit runtime for evaluating autonomous drone agents in simulation, SITL, and bench-preparation workflows.

The demo:

- agent attempts unsafe MAVLink-style command
- policy denies the command
- replay verifies the audit trail
- safety-case report explains the rule, decision, evidence, and limitations
- red-team scorecard captures adversarial/fault scenarios

Best fit: teams using PX4, ArduPilot, MAVLink, ROS2/MAVROS, companion computers, autonomy planners, or SITL workflows who need better safety evidence before deeper integration.

Boundary: no aircraft operation, no certification, no approval claim, no detect-and-avoid claim, no substitute for customer safety process.

If this maps to your workflow, I would like to run a 20-minute technical demo.
