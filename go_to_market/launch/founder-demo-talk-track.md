# Founder Demo Talk Track

## 30 Seconds

"Aegis Edge sits between autonomy software and a supported command bridge during evaluation. It checks commands against safety policy, records audit/replay evidence, runs red-team scenarios, and generates a safety-case report."

## Safety Boundary

"This is evaluation evidence only: fake adapter, SITL, or no-actuation bench-preparation. It is not aircraft operation, approval, detect-and-avoid, or a substitute for your safety process."

## Demo Narrative

1. "Here is the policy envelope: geofence, altitude, velocity, battery, approval, data, and health rules."
2. "Here is the agent trying a command outside that envelope."
3. "Aegis denies it and records the exact reason."
4. "Replay verifies the audit trail."
5. "The safety-case report packages the decision, evidence, and limitations."
6. "The red-team scorecard shows which scenarios passed, failed, skipped, or need support."

## Close

"The pilot question is not 'does this solve all safety?' The pilot question is: can we make one autonomy command workflow more reviewable, testable, and evidence-backed in your simulator?"
