# First 50-Account Build Guide

## Rule

Use public company information, warm introductions, and user-supplied business context only. Do not collect private personal data or use bulk account harvesting.

## Target Categories

- YC drone/autonomy companies.
- Drone inspection companies.
- Warehouse drone companies.
- Agriculture drone companies.
- Public safety drone companies.
- Autonomous fleet companies.
- Robotics companies using MAVLink or ROS2.
- Companies with visible SITL, PX4, ArduPilot, or MAVLink usage.

## Research Signals

- Mentions PX4.
- Mentions ArduPilot.
- Mentions MAVLink.
- Mentions ROS2/MAVROS.
- Mentions companion computer.
- Mentions autonomy.
- Mentions geofencing.
- Mentions BVLOS as a customer pressure, not as an Aegis claim.
- Mentions safety case.
- Mentions fleet operations.
- Hiring for autonomy, robotics, simulation, platform, field reliability, or safety.

## Build Process

1. Create 8-10 accounts per category.
2. Record public stack evidence.
3. Score with the qualification framework.
4. Run the customer safety filter.
5. Prioritize accounts with simulator signals and safety/evidence pain.
6. Ask for warm intros before cold outreach when possible.

## Do Not Add

- Private phone numbers.
- Personal email addresses not shared for business outreach.
- Non-public internal stack details.
- Classified/export-controlled project notes.
- Any real secret, token, or credential.
