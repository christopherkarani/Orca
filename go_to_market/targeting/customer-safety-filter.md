# Customer Safety Filter

## Disqualify Or Require Extra Review

- weapons/kinetic drone use
- offensive military use
- surveillance-heavy use without clear compliance
- customers asking for real-flight shortcuts
- customers asking to bypass failsafes
- customers asking for certification claims
- customers unwilling to use simulation/SITL first
- customers asking for private-data collection outside the evaluation scope
- customers asking to hide limitations or unsupported results

## Pass

- Commercial autonomy team.
- Simulator or SITL workflow.
- Technical owner available.
- Clear command surface.
- Wants audit/replay, policy evidence, red-team scenarios, or customer proof.
- Accepts evaluation-only boundary.

## Review

- Defense-adjacent but non-kinetic use.
- Public-safety use with clear compliance process.
- Custom messages or unusual command bridge.
- Hardware bench request that can remain no-actuation.

## Required Note

If a customer fails this filter, do not proceed with outreach or pilot scoping until the founder explicitly reviews the account.
