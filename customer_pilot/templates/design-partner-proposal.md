# Design Partner Proposal

Draft template. Legal, safety, and commercial review required before external use.

## Problem Statement

Drone and autonomy teams need a practical way to evaluate policy-mediated command execution, safety-envelope decisions, audit/replay evidence, red-team scenarios, and safety-case reporting before deeper integration work.

## Why Now

Autonomy stacks are moving faster than safety-review tooling. A local evaluation pilot can identify command-surface gaps, policy gaps, data egress risks, and evidence needs before production planning.

## Proposed Pilot

A two-week simulation/SITL/bench-preparation pilot using fake adapter, PX4 SITL, ArduPilot SITL, or no-actuation bench-preparation evidence.

## Deliverables

- Stack map.
- Command surface inventory.
- Baseline policy.
- Scenario results.
- Red-team scorecard.
- Safety report.
- Evidence bundle.
- Final pilot report.

## Timeline

- Week 0: discovery and scope.
- Week 1: setup and baseline scenarios.
- Week 2: red-team, reporting, and recommendations.

## Required Customer Input

- Autonomy stack overview.
- Autopilot and MAVLink usage.
- Simulator availability.
- Safety constraints.
- Data handling expectations.

## Expected Outcomes

- Clear policy baseline.
- Evidence of deny/allow behavior in scoped evaluation.
- Known limitations.
- Next integration recommendation.

## Safety Boundary

No real flight, live aircraft control, certification, regulatory approval, detect-and-avoid, or autopilot replacement is included.
