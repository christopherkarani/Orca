# Pilot Success Criteria

## Success Criteria

- Edge runs in a customer-like simulation/SITL environment.
- A customer policy baseline is created.
- At least one unsafe command is denied; at least one unsafe command is denied is a required proof point.
- At least one emergency-safe command is allowed/logged according to policy.
- At least one stale-state or health scenario is tested.
- At least one mission/geofence scenario is tested.
- At least one data/telemetry egress scenario is tested.
- The red-team suite runs.
- A safety-case report is generated.
- Audit/replay hash verification succeeds; audit/replay hash verification succeeds is a required proof point.
- Limitations are documented.
- The customer understands next integration steps.

## Non-success Criteria

Non-success criteria:

These are not pilot success measures:

- Real flight.
- Certification.
- Production deployment.
- Full integration with all customer systems.
- Full coverage of all commands or modes.
- Full detect-and-avoid; full detect-and-avoid is not a success measure.
