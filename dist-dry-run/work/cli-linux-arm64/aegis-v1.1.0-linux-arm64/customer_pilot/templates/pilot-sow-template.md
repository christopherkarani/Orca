## Scope

- Simulation/SITL/bench-preparation evaluation of Aegis Edge.
- Baseline policy creation.
- Scenario execution.
- Audit/replay evidence review.
- Red-team scorecard review.
- Safety-report generation.

## Out-of-scope

- Real flight.
- Live aircraft control.
- Propeller, motor, or actuation procedures.
- Certification or regulatory approval.
- Detect-and-avoid.
- Autopilot replacement.
- Hosted telemetry, SaaS, billing, or license enforcement.

## Deliverables

- Stack map.
- Command surface inventory.
- Baseline policy.
- Scenario list.
- Safety report.

- Evidence bundle.
